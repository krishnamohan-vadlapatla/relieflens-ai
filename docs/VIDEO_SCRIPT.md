## Recording Tips

1. **Use a dark, professional background** - conveys seriousness
2. **Speak clearly and slowly** - match the emergency context
3. **Show phone screen clearly** - use a phone frame or screen recording
4. **Add subtle sound effects** - heartbeat, alert sounds (optional)
5. **Use dramatic music** - tense but hopeful tone

## Equipment Needed

- Smartphone with app installed
- Screen recording software
- Telugu-speaking voice actor (or subtitle only)
- Basic video editing (CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, iMovie)
